Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to popular questions about our school

To enroll your child in the school, you need to fill out an application at the principal's office or through the electronic government services portal. Required documents: parent's passport, child's birth certificate, proof of residence, and medical card.

To get access to the electronic gradebook, you need to contact the homeroom teacher to get a login and initial password. After the first login, the system will ask you to change your password. The electronic gradebook is also available through the mobile app.

Our school offers a wide range of extracurricular activities: sports sections (football, basketball, volleyball), creative studios (fine arts, music, theater), science clubs (robotics, programming, engineering design), and language courses (English, German, French). A detailed schedule is available in the "Extracurricular Activities" section on our website.
