About Invatamint Dual

Invatamint Dual is a modern information system designed to organize effective interaction between students, teachers, parents, and administration.

Our portal provides a single point of access to important information and convenient tools for all participants in the educational process.

What we offer:

  • Access to up-to-date information about events and activities
  • Publication of news and announcements
  • Personal accounts for students, teachers, and parents
  • Interaction with partner organizations

Our Mission

Creating a modern educational environment using information technology to improve the quality of education and develop 21st century skills in students.

Information

Improving the accessibility of information for all participants in the educational process.

Interaction

Creating convenient tools for interaction between students, teachers, and parents.

Management

Improving the efficiency of educational process management.

Digitalization

Developing digital skills in students and teachers.
